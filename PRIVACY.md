# Privacy Policy for EchoMe

**Effective Date:** July 1, 2026

EchoMe is an AI companion app that lets you import chat history, create an AI "Echo" from that history, and chat with the generated Echo.

## Information You Provide

EchoMe processes content that you choose to provide, including imported chat logs, participant names extracted from those logs, generated personality profiles, conversation history, and messages that you send to an Echo.

## How Information Is Used

The app uses the information you provide to analyze chat history, create personality profiles, store local Echo records, retrieve relevant memories, and generate AI chat responses.

## AI Processing

EchoMe uses third-party AI services, such as Google Gemini, to analyze chat content and generate responses. Content needed for these features may be sent to the selected AI provider for processing. Review the provider's privacy terms to understand how they handle submitted content.

## Local Storage

Echo profiles, memories, and conversation history may be stored locally on your device using the app database. You can delete Echo records from within the app where supported.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share your content with advertisers. Data is shared with third-party AI providers only as needed to provide the AI features you request, or when required by law.

## Security

No transmission or storage method is perfectly secure. We use reasonable safeguards appropriate for an app of this type, and you should avoid importing chat logs that you do not have permission to use.

## Children

EchoMe is not intended for children under 13. Do not use the app to process children's personal information.
